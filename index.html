Why we built this

The two big Claude groups bury people. You write a real question, a normal one, and it vanishes into an approval void. The only posts that ever survive are the loud ones. After a while you stop asking, because what's the point if it never sees daylight.

So we built the opposite. A place where a normal person asks a normal question and gets a real answer, in public, where it can't be silently deleted. Nobody quietly makes your post disappear. We don't bury real people. That's the one rule we'll never break.

Who it's for

Three kinds of people show up here, and they all get the same thing: honest help, no hype.

The builder
One person and a laptop
You're trying to make a real thing. You want honest help and a straight answer about what works, not a hype reel from someone selling a course.
The operator
Runs a business or ads
You're tired of guru advice from people who don't actually run ads. You want a method and real numbers, the kind you can check against your own.
The parent or teen
Build, don't just chat
You want a kid to build something with AI, not just talk to it. You're not technical, and you want a real on-ramp that doesn't assume you are.
