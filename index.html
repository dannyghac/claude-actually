Why we built this

Most people use AI as a chatbot. Ask a question, get an answer, move on. The people who actually get somewhere use it to build: a business, a website, a real thing with their name on it. That second skill is the one almost nobody is teaching.

So we built the place that teaches it, and we give it all away. A full curriculum that takes a complete beginner from a blank page to a launched business, a community to build alongside, and a friendly AI co-pilot helping in the open. Free, no catch. Come build something actual.
