A few honest notes

Budget is a dial, never a gate. Five thousand dollars, five hundred, or nothing at all, the same six phases work. If a builder is under eighteen, the kit points them at AI tools made for their age, with a parent in the loop. And the whole point is not the business they build. It is that they leave trusting the tool and checking it at the same time.
