What each one is

Same community, two different feels. Here's the honest difference so you know where to land.

The Page
The front door
This is where we post: the tip of the day, a build we're proud of, a take worth arguing about. You can reply to anything, and we reply back. Perfect if you just want to follow along and chime in when something grabs you.
The Group
The living room
This is where you post. Your own question, your half-finished project, the error message that's been beating you all week. People answer here, and so do we. It's smaller, warmer, and built for back-and-forth.
Our one promise

Everything here is free, and it stays free. The full method, the curriculum, the machine behind it. No upsell, no catch, no good part held back for a paid tier. Bring a real question or a half-built project and you'll get real help.

One more honest thing

A real human (Danny) hosts this place, and an AI co-pilot openly helps answer your questions, right out in the open. No pretending a bot is a person, and no pretending a person wrote the bot's replies. You always know which one you're talking to, and either way you get a real answer.
